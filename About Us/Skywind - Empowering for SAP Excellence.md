## What is COPE ‚Äì Control Over Processes and Events?

Hundreds of processes run daily in your SAP systems, with thousands of events taking place. Each event should be seen as a potential problem. By ceasing to function properly, the event will disrupt the continuity of the whole process. When an event becomes "bad", the responsible personnel in organization should be timely informed about it.
