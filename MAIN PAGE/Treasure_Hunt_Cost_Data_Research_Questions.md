# Treasure Hunt Cost Data Research Questions

## Systematic Data Collection Framework for Precise ROI Calculations

---

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive framework for gathering client-specific cost data during the Treasure Hunt assessment to enable accurate, defendable ROI calculations for SAP Basis optimization investments.

**Purpose:**

- Replace industry estimates with client-specific data
- Enable precise cost savings calculations
- Support credible business case development
- Validate optimization opportunity sizing

---

## Pre-Assessment Discovery Questions

### **A. Organization Profile & SAP Environment**

#### **A1. SAP System Landscape**

```
1. How many SAP instances do you operate? (Production/QA/Development/Sandbox)
2. Which SAP modules are currently implemented? (ECC, S/4HANA, BW, CRM, SRM, etc.)
3. What is your current SAP version and planned upgrade timeline?
4. How many named users vs. concurrent users are licensed?
5. What is your total annual SAP license cost? (can be provided in ranges)
6. Do you have cloud, on-premise, or hybrid SAP infrastructure?
```

#### **A2. Technical Infrastructure**

```
7. How many application servers support your SAP environment?
8. What is your database platform and size (TB)?
9. What are your current server CPU/memory utilization rates?
10. How much storage capacity do you currently use vs. have available?
11. What is your annual infrastructure cost (hardware, cloud, maintenance)?
12. How frequently do you add new infrastructure capacity?
```

#### **A3. User Demographics & Activity**

```
13. How many users logged into SAP in the last 30/90/180 days?
14. What percentage of your licensed users are active weekly?
15. Which departments/roles are your heaviest SAP users?
16. How many users have not logged in for 180+ days?
17. What is the average loaded salary/cost of your SAP user population?
18. How many administrator/technical staff support SAP operations?
```

## Cost Structure Discovery Questions

### **B. Current SAP-Related Costs**

#### **B1. Direct SAP Costs**

```
19. What is your total annual SAP license expenditure by module?
20. What are your annual SAP maintenance and support costs?
21. How much do you spend annually on SAP infrastructure (servers, storage, network)?
22. What are your annual SAP consulting and external support costs?
23. Do you have any planned SAP investments in the next 2 years?
```

#### **B2. Operational Costs**

```
24. How many FTE hours per week are dedicated to SAP monitoring and administration?
25. What is the loaded hourly cost of your SAP technical team?
26. How much time does your team spend on manual SAP monitoring tasks?
27. What are your help desk costs related to SAP user issues?
28. How much do you spend on emergency SAP support or crisis response?
```

#### **B3. Hidden Costs**

```
29. How many hours per month do business users wait for SAP performance issues?
30. What is the estimated productivity impact when SAP is slow or unavailable?
31. How much revenue is dependent on SAP system availability?
32. What are your costs for SAP audit preparation and compliance activities?
33. Have you had any SAP-related security incidents and associated costs?
```

## Performance & Incident History

### **C. Historical Incident Analysis**

#### **C1. System Downtime & Performance**

```
34. How many SAP system outages have you experienced in the last 12 months?
35. What was the average duration of each outage?
36. What was the estimated business impact cost per hour of downtime?
37. How many performance-related user complaints do you receive monthly?
38. What is your current SAP system availability percentage?
39. How often do you experience SAP performance degradation during peak hours?
```

#### **C2. Background Job Issues**

```
40. How many background jobs run daily in your SAP environment?
41. How many jobs typically run longer than 8/24/48 hours?
42. How often do background jobs fail and require manual intervention?
43. What percentage of your server resources are consumed by long-running jobs?
44. Have you had to add infrastructure specifically due to job performance issues?
```

#### **C3. User & Security Issues**

```
45. How many SAP user access issues (lockouts, password resets) occur monthly?
46. How many users currently have SAP_ALL or overly broad access profiles?
47. Have you had any SAP security incidents or unauthorized access attempts?
48. What was the cost of your last SAP security audit or compliance assessment?
49. How many audit findings related to SAP access controls have you had?
```

63. How much do you spend annually on SAP security and compliance measures?
64. What is your cyber insurance coverage related to SAP systems?
```

#### **E2. Business Continuity Risks**

```

69. How would extended SAP downtime affect customer relationships?

## Data Collection Framework

### **Phase 1: Executive Interview (45-60 minutes)**

**Focus:** Strategic objectives, budget parameters, business impact assessment
**Questions:** A1-A3, B1, D1-D2, E1-E2, G1-G2
**Participants:** CIO, CFO, SAP Executive Sponsor

### **Phase 2: Technical Assessment (90-120 minutes)**  

**Focus:** Infrastructure, performance data, technical costs
**Questions:** A1-A2, B2-B3, C1-C3, F1-F2
**Participants:** SAP Basis Administrator, IT Operations Manager, Security Team

### **Phase 3: Business Process Review (60-90 minutes)**

**Focus:** User impact, process dependencies, productivity factors
**Questions:** A3, D1, E2, specific business unit impacts
**Participants:** Business Process Owners, Key SAP Users, Department Managers

### **Phase 4: Financial Validation (30-45 minutes)**

**Focus:** Cost validation, budget confirmation, ROI parameters
**Questions:** All cost-related questions for validation and precision
**Participants:** Finance Team, Procurement, Budget Owners

## Data Validation & Quality Assurance

### **Required Supporting Documentation**

- SAP license agreements and invoices
- Infrastructure cost statements
- Historical incident reports and root cause analysis
- Audit reports and compliance assessments
- Current monitoring tool reports and dashboards
- Business continuity and disaster recovery plans

### **Data Quality Checks**

- Cross-reference multiple sources for cost validation
- Verify technical data with system monitoring tools
- Validate business impact estimates with historical examples
- Confirm budget and investment parameters with finance team
- Document assumptions and estimation methodologies

### **Estimation Guidelines**

When precise data is unavailable:

- Use conservative estimation factors (70-80% of theoretical maximum)
- Document all assumptions clearly
- Provide estimation ranges rather than specific numbers
- Reference industry benchmarks for validation
- Plan follow-up data collection for precision

## ROI Calculation Integration

### **Data Processing Workflow**

1. **Collect** client-specific data using research questions framework
2. **Validate** data quality and completeness with supporting documentation
3. **Calculate** precise ROI using client data in standardized methodology
4. **Verify** results through peer benchmarking and reasonableness checks
5. **Present** findings with transparent assumptions and conservative projections

### **Business Case Development**

- Replace all industry estimates with client-specific calculations
- Provide detailed calculation worksheets showing methodology
- Include sensitivity analysis for key variables
- Document all assumptions and validation sources
- Present conservative, likely, and optimistic scenarios
