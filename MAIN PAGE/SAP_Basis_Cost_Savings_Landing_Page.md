#### **Challenge: Long-Running Job Resource Drain**

**Current Resource Waste:**

- Jobs running 24+ hours abnormally consuming excessive CPU, memory, I/O resources
- Database bottlenecks from inefficient job processing causing system-wide performance degradation  
- Job queue congestion blocking critical business processes
- Resource-intensive operations preventing optimal system utilization

**Resource Optimization Impact:**

- **Up to 80% improvement in job operating efficiency²** through intelligent job analysis and optimization
- **Significant hardware cost avoidance** by eliminating need for additional server capacity (varies by current utilization)
- **25-40% reduction in processing time²** through automated resource allocation optimization
- **Maintains critical business operations** without resource contention interruptions

**ROI Estimation:** *Large organizations with 1,000+ daily background jobs may achieve substantial annual savings through optimized resource utilization (client-specific assessment required for precise calculations)*

#### **Challenge: Inactive User License Waste**

**Exception Indicator: Inactive Users Monitoring (SW_01_20_NOTACT_USER)**

**Current Resource Waste:**

- Dormant accounts (180+ days inactive) consuming unnecessary expensive SAP licenses
- Systematic accumulation of inactive accounts with retained access permissions
- License compliance violations from poor user account hygiene
- Administrative overhead from manual inactive user management

**License Optimization Impact:**

- **$5,000-$50,000+ annual savings per removed inactive license³** (based on standard SAP licensing costs, varies by module mix)
- **Up to 35% decrease in unauthorized access attempts²** through systematic dormant account elimination
- **40-60% reduction in manual account management workload²** through automated inactive user inventory
- **Compliance assurance** preventing regulatory violations (potential fine amounts vary by jurisdiction and industry)

**ROI Estimation:** *Organizations with significant inactive user populations may achieve substantial annual license cost recovery (requires license audit for precise calculations)*

- Emergency resource procurement due to poor capacity planning
