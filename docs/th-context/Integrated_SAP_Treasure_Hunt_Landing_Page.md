## What Skywind Detects During Treasure Hunt - Examples

*The following examples represent excerpts from Skywind's AI-trained detection capabilities, where algorithms educated on thousands of SAP environments automatically identify critical risks, with each finding validated by experienced SAP experts.*

### **Business Protection**

- **Material Conversion Fraud**: High-value materials converted to low-value items (convert a car to a pencil) enabling merchandise theft
- **Vendor Payment Diversion**: Bank account changes followed by quick reversals to redirect payments fraudulently
- **Backdated Purchase Orders**: Purchase orders created after vendor invoice date to bypass approval controls
- **One-Time Vendor Abuse**: New temporary vendors created with regular vendor bank details for payment fraud

### **Access Governance**

- **Critical System Access Violations**: Users granted dangerous system-wide access permissions (SAP_ALL profiles)
- **Unauthorized Access Escalation**: Users gaining elevated privileges without proper authorization
- **Vendor Payment Control Fraud**: Same user controlling vendor setup AND payment processing enabling fake invoice schemes
- **Self-Approval Violations**: Purchase orders and financial documents approved by their creators

### **Business Control**

- **Stuck Purchase Orders**: Procurement processes stalled over 60 days causing operational bottlenecks
- **Data Exchange Failures**: Critical business data transmission breakdowns affecting operations (200+ failures in 10 minutes)
- **Unbilled Deliveries**: Goods shipped but not invoiced causing revenue recognition delays and cash flow loss
- **Incomplete Service Orders**: Uncompleted maintenance and customer service work affecting productivity
- **Suspicious Financial Postings**: Unusual accounting entries deviating from normal business patterns

### **Technical Control**

- **System Dumps Monitoring**: ABAP runtime errors (300+ dumps in 10 minutes threshold) indicating system issues
- **Application Lock Conflicts**: Prolonged locks (10+ hours) causing performance bottlenecks
- **Server Resource Control**: CPU (<10% free), memory (<5% RAM), disk space (<10% free) monitoring
- **Update Request Failures**: Database update processing failures (200+ failed requests in 20 minutes threshold)
- **Configuration Drift Detection**: Unauthorized changes to system configurations and transport management

### **Jobs Control**

- **Long-Running Background Jobs**: Jobs running abnormally long (24+ hours) indicating system inefficiency
- **Job Processing Failures**: Failed batch processes (100+ jobs failed in 10 minutes) affecting business operations
- **Resource Utilization Monitoring**: Background job performance analysis and optimization recommendations
- **Job Overlap Detection**: Identifying conflicting or redundant job schedules affecting system performance

### **S/4HANA Excellence**

- **Post-Migration Data Validation**: Comprehensive checks for data consistency after S/4HANA transition
- **Fiori Interface Adoption Monitoring**: User adaptation tracking and support for new interface workflows
- **Real-Time Processing Oversight**: Monitoring instant posting impacts and financial process changes
- **Universal Journal Validation**: Ensuring proper S/4HANA financial posting integrity and compliance
