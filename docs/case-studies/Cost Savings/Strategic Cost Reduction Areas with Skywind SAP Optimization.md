## Specific Alerts and Direct Cost Savings (Mapped to Front-Page Areas)

### Business Control

#### Inventory Management Optimization

**Alert: Suspicious Material movements**

**Cost Saving Scenario:**

- Prevention of theft or unauthorized movement of inventory
- Saves thousands of dollars in lost goods
- Eliminates associated investigation costs
- Reduces insurance claims and shrinkage

**ROI Impact:** Medium-High - Protects physical assets and reduces operational losses

#### Purchasing Process Control

**Alert: Purchase Order approved by creator**

**Cost Saving Scenario:**

- Identifies and prevents self-approved purchase orders
- Eliminates potential overspending or kickback schemes
- Can save hundreds of thousands of dollars annually
- Prevents procurement fraud and vendor collusion

**ROI Impact:** High - Prevents systematic financial leakage

### Business Protection

#### Financial Fraud Prevention

**Alert: Exceptional postings by GL account**

**Cost Saving Scenario:**

- Early detection of fraudulent activities prevents significant financial losses
- Single prevented fraudulent transaction of $100,000 immediately justifies platform investment
- Ongoing protection against internal financial manipulation

**ROI Impact:** High - Direct prevention of financial losses

### Access Governance

#### User Access Control

**Alert: Users having SAP_ALL (or any other) Profile**

**Cost Saving Scenario:**

- Detects and rectifies overly permissive user access
- Prevents costly data breaches or internal fraud
- Avoids regulatory fines and compliance violations
- Potential savings in millions for prevented major security incidents

**ROI Impact:** Very High - Prevents catastrophic security and compliance costs

### Technical Control

#### System Performance Monitoring

**Alert: Servers CPU controlling (Free CPU < 10%)**

**Cost Saving Scenario:**

- Proactively addresses high CPU usage before system crashes
- Prevents hours of downtime and lost productivity
- For large enterprises: potential savings of tens of thousands of dollars per hour
- Avoids emergency hardware purchases and crisis response costs

**ROI Impact:** Very High - Prevents catastrophic downtime costs

### Jobs Control

#### Background Job Optimization

**Alert: Abnormally long running jobs (24+ hours)**

**Cost Saving Scenario:**

- Identifies and optimizes long-running jobs
- Frees up system resources and improves overall performance
- Reduces need for additional hardware investments
- Potential savings of hundreds of thousands of dollars in infrastructure costs

**ROI Impact:** High - Optimizes existing infrastructure investment

### S/4HANA Excellence

- Cross-impact from Background Job Optimization and System Performance Monitoring:
  - Optimized resource utilization
  - Reduced need for additional hardware investments
  - Avoids emergency hardware purchases and crisis response costs
